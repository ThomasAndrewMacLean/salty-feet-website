FREE TRAVELER

How does it work? - Step by step

Free Bird Traveler gives you the freedom to shape your own holiday package. Choose your preferred accommodation, travel dates, and the activities you want to fill your days with. You set the budget and the vibe—we’ve got your back with local tips and guidance, so you won’t waste a minute wondering what’s next. Just dive into that Mediterranean magic and soak up the sun!

Create your holiday package

  1. Choose your dates
    Decide when you want to travel, and how long you'd like to stay.
  2. Pick your accomodation
    Select the type of stay that fits your vibe - Hotel or villa
  3. Select your preferred activities
    Fill your holiday with the experiences you want, from diving to yoga and beyond.

Receive an approximate price

After submitting your choices, you’ll get an estimated price.

  1. Send your inquiry
    We’ll get back to you within 3 days via email with a detailed price and available accommodation options.

Confirm your package

Finalize your selection and confirm your booking.

  1. Secure your spot
    Make a 20% prepayment of the total price to lock in your holiday.
  2. Pay the rest on arrival
    Settle the remaining balance when you arrive at your stay.
