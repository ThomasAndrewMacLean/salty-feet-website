How does it work?

When planning your trip, you have two options: Social Traveler or Free Birds.

Social Traveler is a retreat with fixed dates each month. Salty Feet organizes daily group activities, giving you a chance to connect with others while still having free time to spend however you like.

Free Birds offers more flexibility. You choose your travel dates and group size, then customize your schedule with activities that suit your preferences.

What does your package look like?

  • With Social Traveler, daily group activities are already planned and included in the price. For the remaining time, you’re free to decide how you’d like to spend your day. If you need suggestions or guidance, we’re here to help you find the perfect activities. Scroll down to see the group activities included in price and explore other experiences available around town.
    "Additional desired activities can also be booked upon arrival in town."
  • The price varies depending on your room choice — shared or private — and your airport pick-up location. If you’re joining the retreat with a friend, we offer a special rate for sharing a room with your travel buddy. We help you find the best flight and arrival airport according to your preferences! Return flights start from 150 euros from Brussels
  • After you confirm your preferred retreat dates, we’ll finalize your booking within a week and request a prepayment to secure your spot. Go to the FAQ to learn more about getting there, prepayment and cancellation policies, activities, and more.

What is included?

  • Accommodation
  • Breakfast
  • PADI certification and training
  • Group activities
  • Airport pick-up and drop-off
